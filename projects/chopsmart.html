ChopSmart

Iteration with Intention Fall 2024

Pair Project Elmer Handojo - Background in Industrial Design & Sustainability

ChopSmart is a smart modular cutting board designed to make cooking at home approachable for young adults living alone. It tackles key challenges like time constraints, cleanliness, and inefficiency during cooking. With features like recipe generation based on ingredients on hand, time, or skill level, ChopSmartâ€™s AI module helps create recipes tailored to leftover ingredients, enabling users to adjust and personalise recipes. This fosters creativity in the kitchen and helps reduce food waste by ensuring ingredients are utilised effectively and meals are made for the desired serving size. Additional features include a scale for automatic adjustments and a precision cutting grid, bridging the gap between beginners and cooking, fostering confidence, and simplifying meal preparation.

Ideating

My partner and I explored various topics before deciding to venture further into the world of cooking. However, we realized that cooking at home was a pain point for both of us, particularly as we navigated living on our own for the first time. Both of us had not learned how to cook from our parents and were relying on different digital resources and our own limited knowledge as we prepared meals at home. As we explored this idea, we interviewed other users in our target demographic and realized this was a larger shared experience than we had realized. The main insights from the users interviewed were that they felt that preparing a meal was too time-consuming and felt unintuitive to those who perceived they had lower cooking abilities.
